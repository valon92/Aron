Paper Cup
Size: 7 Oz

A 7oz paper cup is a versatile and eco-friendly option for serving both hot and cold beverages. With a capacity of approximately 200–210 ml , it is ideal for coffee, tea, water, and juices. These cups are commonly used in offices, events, and vending machines due to their convenient size and disposable nature. Made from recyclable materials, they offer a sustainable alternative to plastic cups
